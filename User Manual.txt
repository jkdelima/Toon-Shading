Assignment 2 User Manual

Follow the steps below to use the program:

1.	Extract the contents of the PA-2 zipped folder into a desired location.
2.	Open the terminal and navigate to the folder location.
3.	Enter the command "./run" and a new window will popup with a silhouette image of a bunny.
4.	Hit the keys 1-4 for a different background.
5.	You can just close the window when you are done.
